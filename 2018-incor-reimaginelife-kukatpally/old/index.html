Five ways in which your life will be different

A life with 5 acres of open spaces
Next

Out of 11.12 acres, 5 acres is used for greenery, gardens, and outdoor activities for the whole family.

The micro-climate of One City is planned to have greenery and flowers in all seasons, and is inspired by an English Country Garden.

Avoid Water Shortages with Intelligent Water Management
Next

Did you ever get stuck in the shower with no water? Not at One City. A number of facilities aimed to conserve water, including municipal water supply, bore water, and several water conservation measures like a water recycling facility, a rainwater harvesting facility and a sewage treatment plant. In times of acute city-wide shortage, a property care team will arrange water supply. Now, your family need not ever face unpredictable water shortages, even during those dry summer days.

100% Power Backup, even in your flat
Next

Imagine a power cut on a hot summer day. Extremely uncomfortable, right? One City provides a 100% power backup with well-supplied diesel generators for all appliances and rooms in your flat, not just common areas. Now you can continue to sleep in peace, even during power cuts on those uncomfortably hot summer nights.

Piped Gas gives uninterrupted gas supply
Next

Have you had those days when you started off cooking a healthy meal at home but ended up ordering from a hotel because the gas ran out? One City has a centralised gas bank and a continuous piped gas supply to every flat. Now, there will be no more endless hassles over booking cylinders.

Always Connected. Without messy wires!
Next

An internet cable here, a TV wire there - how many of us have had the beauty of our houses destroyed by a messy bunch of wires? This is why One City has centralised DTH and Broadband. All the cables and wires will be installed and distributed centrally across the township so that the beauty of your home is preserved!.

Dedicated Service Team, for those rare breakdowns
Next

Plumbing problems, maintenance issues? These are solved quickly at One City. Our dedicated Property Care representatives are always at your beck and call to address all your grievances, concerns and issues regarding maintenance. A help-desk app allows you to report problems and track their status until resolution.
